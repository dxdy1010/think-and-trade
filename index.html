Core Philosophy

We teach algorithmic price delivery — a disciplined, rules-based approach that identifies liquidity-driven moves and trades only when high-probability conditions align. This reduces emotion, enforces risk control, and prioritizes precision over frequency.

Algorithmic price movement

Markets move in measurable patterns driven by liquidity and large participants. We map those conditions and trade when the structure is in our favor.

Buyside vs sellside runs

We identify when price is preparing for a buyside (up) or sellside (down) liquidity run, and align entries accordingly.

Precision > Frequency

We do not trade every day. We trade the right setups and let compounding produce results over time.
